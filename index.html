NHL Goal Distribution per minute by team

It was the 2013-2014 NHL season and it felt like every other game the Red Wings played, they gave up a goal in the last minute of the game, mostly to lose the game or have the opponent force overtime.

With that, I looked to see just how often this happened with Detroit, and every team in the NHL.

A few findings in the data

  • The Red Wings did in fact give up the most goals in the last minute of the game (-10).
  • About a third of the teams, even ones with a large negative differential, have a positive progression of goals in the last three to four minutes of regulation.
  • For a large number of teams, there are very few goals for or against in the first minute of a game, but often the opposite in the last minute of the game.

Data from NHL.com.

* SO - A shootout win counts as a goal for for the winning team and a goal against for the losing team.
